Beyond Cricket: The Empire of Virat Kohli

The Businessman

The things Virat Kohli did on the pitch will go down in history. But what many people ignore is how he used his fame off the field — building an incredible business empire. Here are some amazing facts about it!

Off the field, Kohli runs over six fast-growing brands — from fitness to fashion, each one climbing toward success.

Kohli is one of the most marketable athletes in the world, endorsing top global brands across sports, fashion, tech, and wellness.

The Athlete

Virat Kohli's fitness is pure discipline turned into legacy. He didn't just transform himself — he redefined athletic standards in Indian cricket. Every rep, every run reflects purpose, making him the benchmark for a new generation.
